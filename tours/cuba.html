Cuba

Cuba is one of the most wonderful places for tourists from all over the world. Cuba can be called the brightest pearl of the Caribbean necklace. By purchasing tours to Cuba, you will get a rest on beautiful beaches with white sand and the purest blue water. Cuba is located at the junction of North, Central and South America, on the island of Cuba (the largest in the West Indies), the island of Juventud, as well as on about 1,600 small islands and coral reefs adjacent to them, belonging to the Greater Antilles group. The coast is characterized by deep bays and many convenient coves. The island is framed by reefs and other coral formations.
