Egypt

Rixos Sharm El Sheikh Resort is located in Nabq district. The hotel has free Wi-Fi throughout, a sandy beach, 11 swimming pools, 7 a la carte restaurants and 5 bars. Guests can enjoy the services of a personal butler on their own beach, and gazebos by the pool can be used for an additional fee. All rooms at Rixos Hotel have a balcony or terrace with sea, garden or pool views. Each room here will provide you with a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a wardrobe and a telephone. Some rooms have a spacious seating area.
